What's Bit Hug?

Bit Hug is an interdisciplinary community of individuals from various corners of Latin America: designers, web developers—both front-end and back-end—ranging from newbies to seniors in well-established companies. They have come together, pooling their experiences and a shared desire for continuous learning. The community embarks on projects to both practice and share their knowledge, both theoretically and empirically.

Join Bit Hug, your community just a Bit away.
